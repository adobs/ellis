Search Strategy

We begin a search with the background and context of the role in the company. We do background research on the industry and market conditons of the role to establish an ecosystem of companies and industries that are related to the job and the quality of talent at each company. From there we develop an in-depth search string strategy across multiple sources to find everyone who is going to be relevant in the ecosystem. After examining each profile we hand pick the best and most qualified individuals and find their contact information and social media profiles.

Product Description

Multi-phase research strategy based on client job specification generates a high quality, and highly targeted list of prospective candidates. This data includes name, company, title, email, phone, address and other links to social media profiles and relevant webpages.

Research Technique and Process

Define Domain

We begin by researching the background on an open job and the company/industry then strategically determine which industry segments and specific companies are going to provide richest pool of qualified candidates.

Define Search Strategy and Develop Selection Strings

Next we develop a search strategy encompassing many different dimensions of a profile, not simply restricted to key word hits. This multi-dimensional search tool will qualify and disqualify on as many as 100 specific boolean search terms.

Expert Review

Once our research analysts develop a talent pool, its reviewed by our Executive Team to assure quality and applicability to each individual search.

Contact Build Out

Once the candidate list is properly refined we build out each profile with contact information, professional profiles and social media profiles.

Business Results / Value Proposition

Search Results

  • Faster launch - recruiters can call dozens, and email dozens, more candidates on the first day the search is formally launched.
  • Better quality - professional research provides a greater number of qualified prospective candidates driving better results and broader selection of finalists giving management more, and better, candidates to choose from.
  • Ongoing T3 research for a single high volume role with multiple positions with the same title will provide the recruiting team with the broadest and deepest pool of talent possible.

Team Results

  • Increased recruiting team performance - improving recruiter placements per year, speed to completion, and placement retention rate, due to better overall fit and quality.

Strategic Advantage

  • Methodical research - Research based on strategy and not just available network opens up the potential pool sourced from hundreds to hundreds of millions collectively elevating the talent pool of the company.

Common Problems Address by T3 Research

  • Prolonged search timelines and searches that seem to go on forever
  • Small candidate slates, limiting choices and forcing compromise to fill positions
  • Spikes in search load overloading recruiting team
  • Poor recruiting team retention and morale
  • Poor quality candidate pools
