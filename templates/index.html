Deliverables & Strategy

Consistent Agile Pipeline

Supporting one recruiter or a whole team, we provide a consistent weekly flow of prospective candidates to fuel the search process. This powerful tool allows the recruiting team to focus their efforts on contact and discussions with qualified candidates instead of endlessly combing the internet. This shift yields faster and more efficient search process and higher quality candidates. We assure minimum numbers of qualified candidates per hour of search, and a detailed target company ecosystem of market research to provide scope context to the market conditions.

Deliverables

  • At least three names with contact information per hour in candidate list or flowchart
  • Ecosystem of market research on target companies
  • Results within 10 days

Deliverables

  • Ecosystem of market research on target companies
  • Results within 14 days

Search Strategy

We begin a search with the background and context of the role in the company. We do background research on the industry and market conditons of the role to establish an ecosystem of companies and industries that are related to the job and the quality of talent at each company. From there we develop an in-depth search string strategy across multiple sources to find everyone who is going to be relevant in the ecosystem. After examining each profile we hand pick the best and most qualified individuals and find their contact information and social media profiles.
